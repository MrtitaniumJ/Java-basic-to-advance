# GeeksforGeeks - Learning, Practice & Interview Preparation

## Platform Overview

GeeksforGeeks (GFG) has evolved into one of the world's most comprehensive platforms combining technical education, problem-solving practice, and career preparation resources. Founded in 2009, it has grown to serve over 10 million monthly visitors, making it one of the largest computer science learning communities. GeeksforGeeks uniquely positions itself as both an educational resource and a practice platform, catering to students, professionals, and those preparing for technical interviews.

The platform distinguishes itself through its vast library of tutorials, in-depth articles, and complementary practice problems. Unlike purely competitive platforms, GeeksforGeeks emphasizes understanding concepts before jumping into problem-solving, making it particularly valuable for beginners and those transitioning between skill levels.

## Dual-Purpose Platform Philosophy

GeeksforGeeks operates on the principle that learning and practice are complementary:

- **Learning First**: Comprehensive tutorials before practice problems
- **Concept Clarity**: Detailed articles explaining theory and implementation
- **Practical Application**: Practice problems reinforcing learned concepts
- **Career Focus**: Interview-specific preparation and guidance
- **Community Driven**: Peer discussions and solution sharing
